TWILIGHT

Synopsis: "Twilight: Breaking Dawn Part 2" is the epic conclusion to the beloved vampire romance saga based on the novels by Stephenie Meyer. Released in 2012 and directed by Bill Condon, the film picks up where the previous installment left off, with Bella Swan, portrayed by Kristen Stewart, embracing her new life as a vampire and mother to her daughter, Renesmee. As the Cullen family bands together to protect their loved ones from the Volturi, who view Renesmee as a threat to vampire law, Bella and Edward, played by Robert Pattinson, face their ultimate test of love and loyalty. With its stunning visuals, gripping action sequences, and emotional depth, "Breaking Dawn Part 2" delivers a thrilling conclusion to the saga, leaving fans on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Actors: Kristen Stewart, Robert Pattinson, Taylor Lautner.

Age Restriction: PG-13
