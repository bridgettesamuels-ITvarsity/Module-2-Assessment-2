STUDENT OF THE YEAR

Synopsis: "Student of the Year" is a vibrant Bollywood coming-of-age film that celebrates friendship, rivalry, and the pursuit of dreams. Directed by Karan Johar and released in 2012, the movie revolves around three students - Rohan, Shanaya, and Abhimanyu, portrayed by newcomers Varun Dhawan, Alia Bhatt, and Sidharth Malhotra, respectively - as they navigate the ups and downs of their lives at St. Teresa's College. Set against the backdrop of high school competitions, romance, and personal ambitions, the trio forms complex relationships and faces intense competition to win the coveted title of Student of the Year. With its colorful musical numbers, glamorous settings, and engaging performances, "Student of the Year" captivates audiences with its blend of youthful energy and heartfelt emotions, making it a beloved favorite among fans of Bollywood cinema.

Actors: Varun dhawan, Alia Bhatt, Sidharth Malhotra.
