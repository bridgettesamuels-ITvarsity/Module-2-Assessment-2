FROZEN

Synopsis: "Frozen" is a heartwarming animated adventure that enchants audiences with its magical tale of love, sisterhood, and self-discovery. Released by Disney Animation Studios in 2013, the film follows the journey of two royal sisters, Elsa and Anna, voiced by Idina Menzel and Kristen Bell respectively, as they navigate the challenges of their kingdom, Arendelle. When Elsa's magical ability to control ice and snow accidentally plunges Arendelle into eternal winter, Anna embarks on a perilous quest to bring her sister back and save their kingdom. Along the way, Anna is joined by a rugged ice harvester named Kristoff, his loyal reindeer Sven, and a hilarious snowman named Olaf, voiced by Josh Gad, who dreams of experiencing the warmth of summer. With its captivating animation, unforgettable songs like "Let It Go," and themes of empowerment and acceptance, "Frozen" has captured the hearts of audiences of all ages, becoming a beloved classic in Disney's storied history.

Actors: Kristen Bell, Idina Menzel, Josh Gad, Jonathan Groff.

Age Restriction: 5+
