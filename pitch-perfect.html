PITCH PERFECT

Synopsis: "Pitch Perfect" is a delightful musical comedy that harmonizes humor, friendship, and the infectious spirit of a cappella singing. Released in 2012 and directed by Jason Moore, the film follows Beca, a rebellious college freshman played by Anna Kendrick, as she reluctantly joins an all-female a cappella group called the Barden Bellas. Led by the determined Chloe, portrayed by Brittany Snow, the Bellas strive to reclaim their former glory and defeat their male rivals, the Treblemakers, in the fiercely competitive world of collegiate a cappella competitions. With its catchy musical performances, witty banter, and ensemble cast featuring Rebel Wilson, Skylar Astin, and Elizabeth Banks, "Pitch Perfect" strikes a perfect chord with audiences, blending toe-tapping tunes and laugh-out-loud moments into a feel-good cinematic symphony.

Actors: Anna Kendrick, Rebel Wilson and Brittany Snow.

Age Restriction: PG-13
